## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 📌 Project 1 — Financial Literacy & Investment Behavior Analysis

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Understanding how individual investors make decisions under     │
│  conditions of uncertainty, risk, and information asymmetry.    │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Overview:** Conducted a structured research study analyzing the investment behavior of retail investors across different demographics, focusing on behavioral biases, risk tolerance, and portfolio diversification patterns.

**Key Contributions:**
- 📊 Designed and administered a structured questionnaire to capture investment preferences, risk profiles, and behavioral tendencies
- 🧮 Applied statistical methods to identify correlations between financial literacy levels and actual investment decisions
- 📉 Evaluated common cognitive biases (anchoring, herd mentality, loss aversion) and their financial impact on retail portfolios
- 📋 Delivered a comprehensive report with policy-level and advisory-level recommendations for improving investor education

**Tools & Skills Used:**
`MS Excel` · `Survey Design` · `Statistical Analysis` · `Research Methodology` · `Report Writing`

**Key Takeaway:** *Investors with higher financial literacy demonstrate meaningfully better diversification habits and lower susceptibility to market panic, highlighting the systemic value of financial education.*

### 📌 Project 2 — Company Financial Analysis: Tata Motors Ltd.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  A deep-dive financial audit of a leading Indian automotive      │
│  conglomerate — evaluating performance, profitability, and       │
│  strategic financial positioning.                                │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Overview:** Performed an end-to-end financial analysis of Tata Motors using publicly available annual reports and financial statements, with the objective of evaluating company health and investment viability.

**Key Contributions:**
- 📁 Extracted and structured 5-year financial data from annual reports for trend analysis
- 📐 Computed and interpreted key financial ratios — Liquidity (Current & Quick Ratio), Profitability (ROE, ROA, EBITDA Margin), Leverage (D/E Ratio), and Efficiency (Asset Turnover)
- 🔄 Conducted DuPont decomposition to assess drivers of return on equity
- 📊 Built a structured Excel dashboard visualizing performance across years with clear narrative commentary
- 🏁 Delivered a SWOT-integrated financial commentary evaluating capital structure and future outlook

**Tools & Skills Used:**
`MS Excel` · `Ratio Analysis` · `Financial Statement Interpretation` · `PowerPoint` · `DuPont Analysis`

**Key Takeaway:** *Tata Motors' JLR segment significantly drives revenue volatility, and the company's leverage position requires close monitoring — findings validated against analyst reports for calibration.*

### 📌 Project 3 — Business Case Study: Starbucks Global Expansion Strategy

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Unpacking the financial and strategic calculus behind           │
│  Starbucks' international market entry and growth decisions.     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Overview:** Developed a full business case study examining Starbucks' international expansion strategy — particularly in Asian markets — analyzing the financial implications, competitive dynamics, and strategic trade-offs involved.

**Key Contributions:**
- 🌍 Mapped Starbucks' market entry modes (Joint Ventures, Licensing, Wholly-Owned Subsidiaries) and evaluated financial risk profiles for each
- 💹 Analyzed revenue contribution by geography and identified high-growth opportunity markets using macro indicators
- 🏪 Assessed unit economics: store-level profitability, capex requirements, payback periods, and franchise vs. owned model comparisons
- 📊 Built a scenario analysis model projecting 3-year revenue implications of aggressive vs. conservative expansion strategies
- 🎯 Presented strategic recommendations with clear financial justification using the BCG Matrix and Ansoff Growth Framework

**Tools & Skills Used:**
`MS Excel` · `Scenario Analysis` · `Strategic Frameworks` · `PowerPoint` · `Business Research`

**Key Takeaway:** *Starbucks' premium positioning acts as both an asset and a constraint in price-sensitive markets — effective localization paired with premium experience delivery is the key financial lever for sustainable international growth.*
